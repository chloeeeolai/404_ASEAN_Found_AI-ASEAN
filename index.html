Key Features

Designed for accessibility, cultural pride, and community empowerment.

🎙

Voice-Enabled Chat

Users can speak questions in their dialect. SEA-LION understands, translates, and responds in language they know.

🌐

Dialect Translation

Bidirectional translation between local dialects and standard languages makes complex government information accessible.

📚

Community Education

Health, finance, farming, and digital literacy content presented in simple, culturally relevant language.

📶

Low-Bandwidth Mode

Works in poor connectivity areas with offline caching, reduced animations, and pre-stored common responses.

🏛️

Cultural Preservation

Community-driven archive of dialect recordings, vocabulary, and traditional stories to prevent linguistic extinction.

🤝

Community-First

Built with input from linguists, rural communities, and cultural organizations to ensure relevance and accuracy.
